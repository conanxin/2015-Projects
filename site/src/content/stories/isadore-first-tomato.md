This story anchors the **public oral history pilot** of 2015 Projects Reborn /
个人知识与记忆实验室. The goal of this pilot is to validate, end-to-end, the
smallest useful unit of the V0.3 family oral-history workflow:

- one narrator,
- one interview (a 41.8-second NPS clip, not a full two-hour session),
- one story page,
- seven chapter timecodes a reader can jump to,
- a pure-frontend chapter search,
- and a clear separation between **nav-only short excerpts** (kept here) and
  the **full transcript** (kept only on the official NPS source page, never in
  this repository).

This is the *product-model* validation, not a content contribution to the
Ellis Island archive.
